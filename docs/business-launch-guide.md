# Dreamlink — Business Launch Guide

*For Justin — a non-technical overview of what's built, what's left, and what it costs.*

---

## What Dreamlink Does (Today)

Dreamlink is an AI-powered Christian dream journal. Users write down their dreams and get:

1. **AI Dream Analysis** — An OpenAI model reads the dream and returns a structured interpretation with themes, symbolism, and a personalized summary
2. **Biblical References** — The AI extracts relevant Bible verses tied to the dream's themes
3. **AI-Generated Dream Art** — Each dream gets a unique image generated by FLUX (Black Forest Labs), styled like a 35mm film photograph
4. **Reading Levels** — Users choose how deep the analysis goes (simple through scholarly)
5. **Dream Journal** — All dreams are saved with search, sharing, and a visual gallery

**Authentication:** Email/password and Google sign-in are both working.

## What's Left Before Launch

### 1. Stripe Integration (Payments)

**What it is:** Stripe is the payment processor — it handles credit cards and subscriptions.

**Current state:** The database tables for subscriptions and payments already exist. The code to actually charge people does not.

**What needs to be built:**
- Checkout flow (user clicks "Subscribe" → Stripe payment page → back to app)
- Webhook handler (Stripe tells our app when someone pays, cancels, or their card fails)
- Usage enforcement (actually limiting free users to 5 analyses/month)
- Trial period logic (the app advertises a 7-day free trial but doesn't enforce it)
- Customer portal (let users manage their own subscription, update payment method, cancel)

**What you need to set up on Stripe's side:**
- Create a Stripe account at stripe.com
- You do NOT need an LLC to start — Stripe supports sole proprietors
- You'll need: a bank account, SSN (or EIN if you have a business), and a government ID
- Verification is usually same-day
- Create three "Products" in Stripe matching the tiers above
- No monthly fees from Stripe — they take **2.9% + $0.30 per transaction**

**Timeline estimate:** This is the biggest remaining engineering task.

---

### 2. Google OAuth Verification

**What it is:** Right now, when users sign in with Google, they see a scary "This app isn't verified" warning screen. Google requires apps to go through a verification process before that goes away.

**What you need to do:**
- Submit for verification in Google Cloud Console (free)
- You'll need a privacy policy URL and terms of service URL on the site
- Takes a few days to a few weeks for Google to review
- No cost

---

### 3. Domain & DNS

**Current state:** The app is live at `dreamlink-iota.vercel.app` (Vercel's free subdomain).

**What you need:**
- Buy a custom domain (e.g., `dreamlink.app` or `dreamlinkjournal.com`)
- Cost: **~$10-15/year** for a .com, ~$12-18/year for .app
- Recommended registrar: Cloudflare (sells at cost, no markup)
- Point it to Vercel (free, automatic SSL)

---

### 4. Privacy Policy & Terms of Service

Required for:
- Google OAuth verification
- App Store listing (if you go mobile later)
- General legal protection
- Stripe compliance

You can use a generator like Termly or iubenda (~$10-50/year) or have a lawyer draft them.

---

### 5. Analytics & Monitoring

Currently no analytics. Consider adding:
- **Vercel Analytics** (included with Pro plan) — page views, performance
- **PostHog** or **Mixpanel** (free tiers available) — user behavior, funnel tracking
- **Sentry** (free tier) — error monitoring so you know when things break

### Revenue vs. Cost at 100 Users (Example Mix)

If out of 100 users: 60 are free, 30 are Visionary ($9), 10 are Prophet ($29):

- **Monthly revenue:** (30 x $9) + (10 x $29) = **$560/mo**
- **Stripe fees:** ~$560 x 3.2% = ~$18
- **Expenses:** ~$136-436/mo
- **Net:** ~$106-406/mo profit

**The biggest lever for profitability is OpenAI cost.** Switching from the current reasoning model to a simpler one (like gpt-4o-mini) could cut AI costs by 50-80%.

## Business Entity

You don't need an LLC to start accepting payments through Stripe (sole proprietor works). However, once you're generating revenue, consider:

- **LLC** — Separates personal liability from the business. Costs ~$50-500 depending on state. Recommended before any significant revenue.
- **EIN** — Free from the IRS, takes 5 minutes online. Needed for business bank accounts and tax filing.
- **Business bank account** — Keeps finances separate. Most banks offer free business checking.
